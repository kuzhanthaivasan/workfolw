Ticket Categorization
Auto or Manual The system automatically categorizes tickets based on rules or allows manual assignment.

Ticket Assignment
Assign to team/individual Tickets are assigned to the most suitable team or person based on expertise and workload.
↓
Acknowledgment Sent
Notify user with Ticket ID Users receive an acknowledgment email with their ticket ID and expected resolution time.
↓
Work on Ticket
Updates added regularly The assigned team works on the ticket, providing status updates and resolving issues promptly.
↓
Resolve or Escalate?
Decision point If unresolved within SLA, the ticket is escalated to a higher authority for faster resolution.
↓
Resolution Confirmation
User notified Users are informed of the resolution and asked for confirmation or feedback.
↓
Close Ticket
Confirmed or time-out Tickets are closed after user confirmation or if no feedback is received within a set time.
↓
Generate Reports
For analytics Data from tickets is compiled into reports to identify trends and improve performance.
